⚙️ Model Architecture
RANDOM FOREST CLASSIFIER

Task: Binary classification — predicts whether a (station, hour, vehicle, day) combination is HIGH-RISK.

Hyperparameters: n_estimators=150, max_depth=12, min_samples_split=10, class_weight=balanced

Features: station, vehicle type, violation type, hour, day of week, month, weekend flag, peak-morning, peak-evening, peak flags

Training: 80/20 stratified train-test split.
